Official app information

Five local party games. One app. No login required.

Life of the Party is an adult party-game collection for in-person group play. Version 1.0 ships with only the five local offline modes listed below.

Get support Read the privacy policy

What ships in 1.0

  • First To Last To
  • Card Roulette
  • Guess That Word
  • Alphabet Game
  • Sing That Song

What to expect

  • No account creation or sign-in.
  • No ads, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.
  • No online multiplayer in the shipped runtime.
  • No camera, microphone, or photo-library prompts in normal play.
