Five local party games. One app for your next group night.

Life of the Party is an adult party-game collection for in-person group play. The current App Store release includes the five local game modes listed below.

How the app is designed

  • Quick local rounds that are easy to start from the home screen.
  • One-device party play for in-person groups.
  • Flexible setup options for each game mode.
  • Built-in support and privacy information in the app.
