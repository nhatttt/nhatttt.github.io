Help for Life of the Party

Life of the Party is designed for fast local play on one device. The shipped 1.0 app does not require login, purchases, or online setup.

Open a support request Privacy Policy

Quick troubleshooting

  • Close and reopen the app if a round screen gets out of sync.
  • Restart the round if you want a fresh local setup state.
  • Make sure motion controls are allowed at the device level for Guess That Word.
  • Update to the latest App Store version before reporting a bug.

Current shipped scope

  • First To Last To
  • Card Roulette
  • Guess That Word
  • Alphabet Game
  • Sing That Song

Before you contact support

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, the game mode you were using, and the exact steps that led to the issue. A screenshot of the affected screen is also helpful when available.

The App Store release path is fully local and offline-first. The shipped runtime does not include Soulstealer, purchases, account creation, or replay recording.

Support contact path

Primary support channel Submit a request through the public GitHub support tracker: github.com/nhatttt/nhatttt.github.io/issues/new
Response scope Best-effort support for bugs, gameplay issues, App Store download problems, and clarification about shipped 1.0 features.
Privacy questions Please refer to the Privacy Policy and include your question in the support request if you need follow-up.
