Life of the Party Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026. This policy applies to the shipped App Store version of Life of the Party.

Summary

  • Life of the Party does not require an account or login.
  • We do not use your data for tracking.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Gameplay preferences stay on your device.
  • Life of the Party is designed for local offline play on one device.

Information handled on your device

  • Local preferences such as recent game routing and saved setup choices.
  • Motion sensor input used during Guess That Word tilt and flip gameplay.
  • Audio playback for local sound effects and timers.

How data is used

  • Local gameplay preferences help reopen the app in the state you expect.
  • Motion sensor input is used only during active Guess That Word gameplay.
  • Audio playback is used for local sound effects and timers.
  • We do not use personal data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data sharing and third parties

Life of the Party is built for local play. Gameplay data stays on your device as part of normal use, and we do not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising purposes.

Data retention and deletion

Life of the Party is built for local offline play and does not maintain user accounts. Local preferences stored on your device can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

Children's privacy

Life of the Party is positioned as an adult party-game app and is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's privacy practices materially change in a future release, this page will be updated before or alongside that release.

Questions

For privacy questions or support follow-up, use the support contact path on the Support page.

This policy is intended to match the App Store Connect privacy answers for the current runtime.
