Life of the Party Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026. This policy applies to the shipped App Store version of Life of the Party.

Summary

  • Life of the Party does not require an account or login.
  • We do not use your data for tracking.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not intentionally collect contact info, photos, videos, audio recordings, identifiers, or diagnostics in the shipped 1.0 app flow.
  • The shipped app is designed for local offline play on one device.

Information handled on your device

  • Local preferences such as recent game routing and saved setup choices.
  • Motion sensor input used during Guess That Word tilt and flip gameplay.
  • Audio playback for local sound effects and timers.

Information we do not collect in 1.0

  • Name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.
  • Photos, videos, microphone recordings, or photo-library content.
  • Precise location, contacts, browsing history, or purchase history.
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data.

Data sharing and third parties

The shipped 1.0 app does not include account services, ad SDKs, analytics SDKs, or in-app purchase flows. Gameplay data is not sent to our servers as part of the normal App Store runtime. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising purposes.

Permissions and archived internal tooling

The public App Store release is aligned to a no-recording, no-camera, no-microphone, and no-photo-library gameplay path. The repository contains archived internal tester-only code related to experimental recording flows, but those features are not part of the default 1.0 App Store runtime.

Data retention and deletion

Because the shipped 1.0 app is built for local offline play and does not maintain user accounts, there is no server-side user profile to delete. Local preferences stored on your device can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

Children's privacy

Life of the Party is positioned as an adult party-game app and is not directed to children.

Changes to this policy

If the app's privacy practices materially change in a future release, this page will be updated before or alongside that release.

Questions

For privacy questions or support follow-up, use the support contact path on the Support page.

This policy is intended to match the App Store Connect privacy answers for the shipped 1.0 runtime.
